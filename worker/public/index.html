Autonomy

How much the System does on its own — one bounded “ponder” each day at 1 pm IST that can research a blocker, surface findings, or nudge you when you're slipping. Every autonomous move is capped (a few a day), respects quiet hours, and is logged with its reasoning in the activity feed. Off: only the morning quests and nightly reckoning. Suggest (default): it researches and messages findings, and proposes plan changes — but never changes your tasks silently. Act: it may also add a task toward your current milestone.

Persona

Who it is when it talks — in Telegram, when it issues your quests, and at the nightly reckoning. The default is The System: a strict mentor. It will not invent facts, fake XP, or claim it did something it didn't, whatever you write here. But tone is yours, and tone carries: ask for gentle and you'll get gentle, even about a slipping streak. "How I see you" takes the name and ignores the voice — it's the one place that stays blunt, because a read you tuned yourself isn't worth reading.

Tell it something

Paste anything — notes, a bio, a job post, a brain-dump. It keeps the facts worth keeping and ignores the rest. Everything it learns here sharpens your quests and its read of you.
